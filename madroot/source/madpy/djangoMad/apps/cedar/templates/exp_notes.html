The Millstone Hill ISR is presently controlled by a control file, as listed below. Lines that begin with a # are comments. Lines that begin 'at [number]' are commands designed to execute at a certain number of seconds. This control file determines which of the two Millstone Hill ISR antennas is active (zenith or the steerable misa), which waveform is used, where the misa antenna is pointing, and how long to integrate for.
